**Better, more accurate answers**

- Sharper retrieval: answers now go through a reranking pass and combine keyword + meaning-based search, so the right passage is found more often — including exact matches like tube types and part numbers (EL34, 6N1P) that meaning-based search alone used to miss.
- Tables are now searchable: tables in your PDFs are pulled out cell-by-cell when indexing, so questions about tabulated values (ratings, specs) hit the actual numbers instead of relying on the page image.
- Honest "not enough data": when your library doesn't clearly cover a question, the answer now carries a low-confidence notice and the model is steered toward saying what's missing rather than guessing.

**See where an answer came from**

- Click a citation to open the cited page with the exact passage highlighted, not just the page.

**Keep your library in sync**

- Edited or replaced a PDF? It's re-indexed automatically; unchanged files are skipped and deleted files are cleaned out. New folder "sync" and "watch" options mirror a folder into the index.

**Share your work**

- Export a whole conversation as Markdown — right-click a chat to copy or save the full transcript, including questions, answers, cited pages, and calculations.

Plus general improvements and bug fixes.
